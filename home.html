    In this portal can add the manager into the portal and can look at the number of managers available in his site. He can also able to look at the user list, design list, current view in his site.     Manager

    Manager should also register before logging into the portal. he is the person who acts as a bridge between the admin and the user. Here the manager will add the new designs into the portal.
